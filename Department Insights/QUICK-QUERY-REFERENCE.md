## 💡 Quick Tips

### Names
✅ Use partial names: "Pratham" not "Pratham Jatilbhai Vora"
✅ Case doesn't matter: "mahima", "MAHIMA", "Mahima"

### Months
✅ Full or short: "August" or "Aug"
✅ Any case: "august", "AUGUST", "August"

### Natural Language
✅ "What is..." ✅ "Show me..." ✅ "How many..."
✅ "Which..." ✅ "Tell me..." ✅ "Compare..."
