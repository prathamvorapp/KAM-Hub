# Quick User Guide: Submit MOM with Product Demos

## What's New? 🎉

You can now record product demo information while submitting your Minutes of Meeting (MOM)! No need to visit the Demos page separately.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Complete Your Visit
- Visit the brand as usual
- Complete your meeting

### Step 2: Click "Submit MOM"
- Go to Dashboard → Visits
- Find your completed visit
- Click the "Submit MOM" button

### Step 3: Fill MOM Details
You'll see 4 tabs in the modal:

#### Tab 1: Manual Entry
- Add action items and open points from the meeting
- At least one open point is required

#### Tab 2: CSV Upload (Optional)
- Upload a CSV file with meeting topics
- Download sample template if needed

#### Tab 3: Product Demos ⭐ NEW!
This is where you record demo information:

**For each product discussed:**

1. **Mark as Applicable**
   - Check the "Applicable" checkbox if you discussed this product
   - If not applicable, provide a reason (e.g., "Already using competitor")

2. **Schedule Demo** (if applicable)
   - Demo Date: When the demo is scheduled
   - Demo Time: Time of the demo
   - Conducted By: Who will give/gave the demo (Agent/RM/MP Training/Product Team)

3. **Mark as Completed** (if demo was given)
   - Check "Mark as Completed"
   - Add completion notes
   - Select conversion status:
     - Converted: Brand agreed to use the product
     - Not Converted: Brand declined
     - Pending Decision: Brand is still thinking
   - If not converted, provide reason

#### Tab 4: Meeting Summary (Optional)
- Add overall meeting notes
- Summarize key discussions

### Step 4: Submit
- Click "Submit MOM & Demos" button
- You'll see confirmation with number of demos recorded

## Tips 💡

### You Don't Need to Fill Everything
- Just mark products as applicable/not applicable
- Fill detailed information only for products you discussed
- You can update demo details later from the Demos page

### Quick Entry During Visit
If you gave demos during the visit itself:
1. Mark product as applicable ✓
2. Fill demo date (today's date)
3. Check "Mark as Completed" ✓
4. Select conversion status
5. Done!

### Schedule for Later
If demo is scheduled for future:
1. Mark product as applicable ✓
2. Fill demo date (future date)
3. Select who will conduct it
4. Leave "Mark as Completed" unchecked
5. Done!

## Benefits

✅ **Save Time**: One form instead of multiple pages  
✅ **Better Memory**: Record while visit is fresh  
✅ **Flexibility**: Fill what you know, update rest later  
✅ **Less Errors**: Won't forget to record demos  

## FAQs

**Q: Do I have to fill all 8 products?**  
A: No! Only fill products you discussed. Others can be left unchecked.

**Q: What if I don't know demo details yet?**  
A: Just mark as applicable. Fill details later from Demos page.

**Q: Can I still use the old way?**  
A: Yes! You can skip the Product Demos tab and fill demos separately later.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**  
A: You can update demo details anytime from the Demos page.

**Q: Do I need to fill demos every time?**  
A: No! Only when you discussed products during the visit.
