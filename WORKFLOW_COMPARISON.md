## 🎯 User Journey Comparison

### Old Journey: Multiple Steps, Multiple Pages

```
Agent → Visits Page → MOM Form → Submit → 
        ↓
        Remember to fill demos → 
        ↓
        Demos Page → Find Brand → 
        ↓
        Fill Product 1 → Fill Product 2 → ... → Fill Product 8 →
        ↓
        Done (if remembered!)
```

**Pain Points:**
- ❌ Easy to forget demo entry
- ❌ Context lost between pages
- ❌ Repetitive navigation
- ❌ Time-consuming
- ❌ Higher error rate

### New Journey: Single Flow, One Page

```
Agent → Visits Page → Enhanced MOM Form →
        ↓
        Tab 1: Action Items →
        Tab 2: CSV Upload (optional) →
        Tab 3: Product Demos →
        Tab 4: Summary (optional) →
        ↓
        Submit → Done!
```

**Benefits:**
- ✅ Can't forget demos (in same form)
- ✅ Context preserved
- ✅ Single navigation flow
- ✅ Time-efficient
- ✅ Lower error rate

## 💡 Real-World Example

### Scenario: Agent visits "Cafe Delight" brand

#### Old Way:
```
9:00 AM - Visit Cafe Delight
10:30 AM - Return to office
11:00 AM - Open Visits page, submit MOM (7 min)
11:07 AM - Get distracted by email
2:00 PM - Remember to fill demos
2:05 PM - Navigate to Demos page
2:10 PM - Find Cafe Delight
2:15 PM - Fill Task demo details
2:20 PM - Fill Purchase demo details
2:25 PM - Fill Payroll demo details
2:30 PM - Done!

Total: 30 minutes (spread across day)
Risk: Almost forgot to fill demos!
```

#### New Way:
```
9:00 AM - Visit Cafe Delight
10:30 AM - Return to office
11:00 AM - Open Visits page, submit MOM
11:02 AM - Fill action items (Tab 1)
11:05 AM - Switch to Product Demos tab (Tab 3)
11:06 AM - Task: ✓ Completed, Converted
11:07 AM - Purchase: ✓ Scheduled for next week
11:08 AM - Payroll: ✗ Not applicable (using Zoho)
11:09 AM - Click Submit
11:09 AM - Done!

Total: 9 minutes (continuous flow)
Risk: Zero - everything in one form!
```
